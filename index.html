Why Now?

Providers are seeing more subsidy gaps, late payments, and enrollment drops as families struggle with affordability. Vilinq keeps tuition continuity and families connected to care.

How Vilinq Works

  1. Provider invites family
  2. Family selects payment option
  3. Vilinq tracks payment schedule
  4. Automated reminders are sent
  5. Provider monitors payment and enrollment status

Why continuity matters

When tuition interruptions happen, children can lose routine and providers face enrollment instability. Keeping payment continuity protects care access and classroom consistency.

How Vilinq reduces disruption

Vilinq helps providers respond early to subsidy delays with flexible plans, bridge options, and proactive reminders so families stay enrolled while assistance catches up.
