Why Now?

Providers are seeing more subsidy gaps, late payments, and enrollment drops as families face growing childcare affordability pressure. Vilinq helps centers proactively support families while protecting tuition continuity and enrollment stability.

How Vilinq Works

  1. Provider invites family
  2. Family selects payment option
  3. Vilinq tracks payment schedule
  4. Automated reminders are sent
  5. Provider monitors payment and enrollment status
